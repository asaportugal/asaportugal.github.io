Sex Addicts Anonymous (SAA) is a fellowship of people for whom sex has become a very serious problem and who meet to help one another. It is a program based on the pioneering model of Alcoholics Anonymous. The only requirement for membership is an honest desire to stop engaging in the destructive patterns of sex addiction that each person identifies.

If you need help, or know someone who needs our support, we recommend that you attend our [meetings]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link m4_meetings.md %}) or [contact](mailto:saalisbon@gmail.com) us. [The international SAA website](https://saa-recovery.org) also contains useful resources (Portuguese can be selected from the top left corner).

We are here to help, because we can only keep what we share.

**For English speakers:** Even if you don't speak Portuguese, you can still join our bilingual Monday (online) and Thursday (in-person) [meetings]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link m4_meetings.md %}), or send us an [e-mail](mailto:saalisbon@gmail.com).
