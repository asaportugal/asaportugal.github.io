### The Discovery

"I am addicted to sex. Before I began recovery, I watched my addiction progress and deeply affect my life. On my own, I couldn’t stop. Going to an SAA meeting was a turning point. With the support of fellow members and by working the steps, I began to recover. Today I remain abstinent from my patterns and continue one day at a time."

### More Than I Ever Dreamed Of

"I entered recovery after years of compulsive behavior, shame, and isolation. I thought life without addiction would be empty. The opposite happened: I regained freedom, healthy relationships, and serenity. Investing in recovery was the best decision of my life."

### A small miracle

“With a period of abstinence, I experienced true sobriety and contact with reality without anesthesia. I realized I don’t need my patterns to cope with life. This was a small miracle that gives me hope to keep going.”

### Together we can

“After many years of using sex as a drug, I found relief through the fellowship and the 12 steps. Today I live with more freedom and gratitude.”

### I finally realized I’m not alone

“For the first time, I found a path of honesty with myself and others. Abstinence has brought clarity, spiritual growth, and hope.”
