Sex Addicts Anonymous (SAA) is a nonprofit fellowship of men and women for whom sex addiction has become a serious problem. We are recovering addicts who meet regularly to help one another and maintain abstinence from our destructive patterns.

There is only one requirement for membership: the desire to stop engaging in the behaviors that each person identifies as destructive throughout recovery. There are no obligations in SAA, but we suggest an open mind to take advantage of this opportunity.

We are not affiliated with any political, religious, or law enforcement organizations. There are no dues or fees to pay. Anyone can join, regardless of age, gender, sexual orientation, beliefs, religion, or lack thereof.

We are not interested in the details of your past. We want to know what you intend to do about your problem and how we can help. The newcomer is the most important person at the meetings, because we can only keep what we share.
